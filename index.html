News & Overview

PrEP-ing at the end of HIV/AIDS is a project funded by Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Individual European Postdoctoral Fellowship. It is funded by the European Union under Horizon Europe (Grant Agreement No. 101200133).

The project investigates the implementation of Combination HIV Prevention and Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP) in Argentina, a comprehensive global strategy of the Joint United Nations Programme on HIV/AIDS (UNAIDS) aimed at eliminating AIDS by 2030. The ethnographic research explores how global-local entanglements involving biotechnologies, institutions, policymakers, health professionals, activists and PrEP users and their experiences, come together in the making of Combination Prevention.

This page will be updated with project news, milestones, and announcements as the research progresses.

This project has received funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie grant agreement No. 101200133.
